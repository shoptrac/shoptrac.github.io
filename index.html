About Us

Brick-and-mortar stores struggle with collecting data on customer traffic, lacking the tools and resources readily available to their e-commerce counterparts. ShopTrac is an all-in-one computer vision solution that allows retailers to count visitors and classify individuals based on age and sex. ShopTrac uses a discreet door-mounted camera system for non-invasive tracking and machine-learning algorithms to profile store customers. With an easy-to-use web dashboard, retailers can know everything about their store performance and customers, right at their fingertips.
